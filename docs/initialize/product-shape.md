# プロダクト形状

この checklist は、技術選定に影響する business shape を定義するためのものです。

## customer model

- b2b
- b2c
- marketplace
- internal tool

## tenancy model

- single-tenant
- multi-tenant
- workspace-based
- organization-based

## identity / permissions

- user-only account
- organization membership
- role
- custom permission
- SSO requirement

## commercial model

- free tier
- trial
- seat-based pricing
- usage-based pricing
- enterprise contract

## data / compliance

- personal data sensitivity
- regional data residency
- audit log requirement
- retention / deletion policy

## decision template

各項目で記録するもの:

- chosen shape
- 影響を受ける feature
- 制約
- 依存する decision
